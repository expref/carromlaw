Privacy Policy

App: Laws of Carrom  |  Effective date: 4 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Laws of Carrom mobile application ("the App") handles user information. The App is provided as a free, offline reference for the official Laws of Carrom published by the International Carrom Federation (ICF).

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to function. Specifically:

  • The App does not request or use your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, microphone, camera, or any other personal data.
  • The App does not require user accounts or sign-in.
  • The App does not contain any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • The App does not transmit any data over the network. It declares no internet permission in its Android manifest.

Local Storage

The App stores certain user preferences (such as bookmarked rules and font-size selection) locally on your device using Android's standard preference storage. This information remains on your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because the App collects no personal data of any kind, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, libraries, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Data Sharing

Because no data is collected, no data is shared with any third party.

Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any user data, there is no personal information at risk. Local preferences stored on your device are protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective date". Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of those changes.
